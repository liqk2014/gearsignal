## Why lighting is a strong affiliate page

Desk lighting is a repeatable setup problem. Buyers are not only searching for
a product; they are trying to make themselves, their workspace, or their product
demo look better on camera.

That lets the site sell the configuration instead of pretending every light is
interchangeable. The best page should explain the minimum useful kit, when to
add a second light, and which accessories prevent clutter.

## What to recommend first

Start with a three-part kit:

- Key light with adjustable brightness and color temperature
- Small accent or background light for depth
- Clamp arm or compact tripod that does not steal desk space

Then add optional upgrades only when they solve a visible problem: diffuser for
glare, USB-C power hub for cable control, or a small product photo mat for
Amazon review content.

## Search angles to write around

This category can support several pages:

- "best desk lighting for video calls"
- "creator desk setup lights"
- "lighting setup for product videos"
- "small YouTube setup at home"
- "ring light vs panel light"

The strongest pages should include real setup photos or diagrams once products
are selected.
