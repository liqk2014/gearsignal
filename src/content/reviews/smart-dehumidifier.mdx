## Why this category is worth building first

Smart dehumidifiers work well for a review-led affiliate page because buyers
arrive with a job, not just curiosity. They want a damp room fixed, a basement
kept stable, or laundry to dry without creating a musty space.

That makes the buying criteria concrete. A useful page can explain room size,
noise, tank capacity, drain hose setup, continuous mode, and whether app control
actually matters.
